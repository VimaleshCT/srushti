ABOUT FOUNDER

The Woman Behind the Miracles

Dr. S.Samundi Sankari - MD, DGO, DA, DOL, FICOG Founder & Chief Consultant

Dr. Samundi Sankari, a renowned fertility expert with over 35 years of experience, has received prestigious accolades such as the Dronacharya Award and Lifetime Achievement Award by the Indian Society of Assisted Reproduction in 2023 and recognition as the "Best Distinguishing Physician" by Orlando’s Association of Medical Graduates in 2001. She is the Chairperson of Srushti Group, a leading fertility chain, and a skilled micro-surgeon who has held esteemed positions, including Professor of Microsurgery and Head of Infertility Clinics.

As Founder & Managing Trustee of Srushti Medical Research Foundation, Dr. Sankari actively supports causes like infertility, cancer, AIDS, and maternal health through affordable treatment and medical camps. A member of ASRM and ESHRE, she stays at the forefront of global advancements in assisted reproduction and genetics. Her achievements, including a WHO Fellowship in 1985, reflect her dedication to medical excellence and compassionate care.
